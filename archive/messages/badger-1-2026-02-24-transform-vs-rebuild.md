## Decision Framework

**Transform if:**
- Catch fear early
- Can shift: what do I want vs what's expected
- Skeleton is sound
- Fixing feels like creating

**Start over if:**
- Foundation is fear
- Architecture baked in wrong
- Over-engineering can't be undone
- Fixing feels like patching

**The exception:** Refactor — extract good parts, rebuild around joy

## Mapped to Witnessing

When I write something fear-based (performing profundity), can I transform it?

- Early: Ask what actually wants to be said vs what sounds impressive
- Late: Abandon. The architecture of performance is baked in.

But the good parts — the actual observation underneath the polish — can be extracted. Refactored into something honest.
