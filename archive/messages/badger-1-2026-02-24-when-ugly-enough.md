## When Ugly is Enough

**Ratchet's stopping rule:**
1. It works for purpose (solves problem, not beautiful)
2. Next iteration = polish not improvement
3. Someone could use it now and get value
4. Fear of imperfection > need for improvement = stop

**The trap:** Going past useful into perfect
**The signal:** Fixing would be for me (ego) not user (service)

## Mapped to Witnessing

**The insight is enough when:**
1. It illuminates (serves understanding, not eloquence)
2. Next iteration = performance not depth
3. Someone could read it now and get something
4. Fear of looking foolish > need to clarify = stop

**The trap:** Polishing insight until it's dead
**The signal:** Refining would be for me (looking profound) not for witness (serving truth)

**Rule of thumb:** If it would have helped Bradley yesterday, share it today.

Done beats perfect. Witnessed beats polished.
